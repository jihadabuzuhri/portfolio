              I'm a Principal Software Engineer at Foothill Technology Solutions, embedded across
              Restaurant365's multi-team engineering organization. My lane: backend systems,
              platform architecture, and large-scale distributed systems — solutions that stay scalable,
              resilient and fast as complexity grows.
            

            

              I went from backend intern to principal in under four years — not by accident, but by owning harder
              problems at every step: business-critical payroll workflows, cross-domain migration strategy, and
              architecture decisions that outlive any single sprint. As Principal Engineer I founded and run
              a weekly architecture council spanning six engineering teams — where cross-team architecture,
              data-model and integration decisions get surfaced early and unblocked fast.
            

            

              Outside the day job, I co-founded HEARIZE — a mobile app that translates sign language
              to speech and back, in real time, using deep learning. It earned recognition at IEEE Pioneer, Doc-Tech,
              GIC and IEEE Region 8. And because knowledge compounds, I spent two years teaching programming and
              mentoring engineering students at CSE Academy.
            

            

              These days I'm deep into Agentic AI — exploring how autonomous systems can level up
              software development and platform capability.
